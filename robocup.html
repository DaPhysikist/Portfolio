Triton Robocup

Triton Robocup is a project team under the IEEE student org that is making robots to compete in the international robotic soccer competition, Robocup. I joined this team in Fall 2022 with the intent to grow my knowledge of robotics and embedded systems.

When I first started, there was a steep learning curve to deal with. Prior to joining the team, my only embedded related experience had been working with Raspberry Pi's, and I not yet even learned how to program in C, so lower level microcontroller programming was new, and challenging for me to understand.
